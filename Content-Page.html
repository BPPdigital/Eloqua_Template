MBA Legal Services

Designed for senior legal managers, BPP University’s MBA Legal Services is a demanding yet rewarding programme which aims to develop and enhance your commercial acumen to take your legal career to the next level.

Building upon your existing legal experience, this programme aims to enrich and improve your knowledge of business and management and specifically demonstrates how to apply this knowledge to legal business.

Why study MBA Legal Services with BPP University?

  • Delivered by BPP University, who exclusively train over 50 leading UK and global law firms’ trainees and deliver programmes to leading companies such as Simmons & Simmons and Lloyds Banking Group.
  • Flexible delivery methods to build your studies around your existing professional life.
  • Opportunity to network with professionals from global law firms.
  • Delivered from our study centre in London City, at the heart of the UK’s Business and Legal Districts.

Programme Details

CIS London & Partners training scheme

CIS London & Partners LLP is a uniquely positioned international law firm based in London. After completing the MBA Legal Services course CIS London & Partners will be exclusively rewarding the top 10 performers* the opportunity to join a unique training scheme which gives you the opportunity to network with legal professionals, join intensive seminars and complete assignments tailored to your field of specialism.
